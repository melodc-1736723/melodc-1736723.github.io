Stream Shine by Pentagon

About Shine

Pentagon will be coming back on April 2nd with the 6th mini album “Positive”. Before that, Pentagon released “Shine spoiler” and 3 short videos, which sparked people’s anticipation towards their new songs. In the first publicized spoiler video, there is an appearance of electric guitar playing the melody of “Shine”. Following in the second publicized spoiler video, Pentagon member Wooseok is featured whistling to the same tune. In the last publicized video, a piano is shown playing the same tune from the previous videos. These unique spoiler videos sparked an intense anticipation towards their new song. With Pentagon’s comeback of the 6th mini album, a refreshing concept, “nerd look” image teasers, and three videos showing the melody, these various comeback spoilers piqued interest among the fans, raising anticipation. The title song “Shine” talks about loving someone but is unable to express it due to a lack of confidence, so the person can only linger around their loved one, making it a song about unrequited love. Through the main piano riff and minimal rhythm, the song gives off a pleasant and bright charm.
